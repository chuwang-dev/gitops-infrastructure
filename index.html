Our Culinary Philosophy

The Sanctuary at GRA, Ikeja

The Sanctuary of Gastronomy

Nestled within the prestigious, tree-lined avenues of GRA, Ikeja (located at 10b Itohan Avenue, off Isaac John), Casi fla stands as a sanctuary of sophisticated gastronomy. Far removed from the bustling energy of the commercial capital, our location was chosen to provide an exclusive, peaceful enclave where fine dining is elevated to an art form.

Our kitchen hosts a respectful dialogue between timeless Nigerian heritage and contemporary culinary techniques. Every dish is a meticulous composition of rare local ingredients, balanced native spices, and avant-garde presentations designed to challenge and delight the senses.

"At Casi fla, the hum of Lagos fades away, replaced by the quiet prestige of an extraordinary culinary escape."
